SEC Edgar Linked Data Wrapper

Welcome to the EXPERIMENTAl SEC Edgar wrapper to Linked Data.

The wrapper currently provides XBRL forms (10-Q, 10-K, 8-K, S4-A, 6-K, 10-Q/A) and form 4.

  • feed/usgaap (from the live Edgar feed)
  • cik/90983#id COSTCO WHOLESALE CORP /NEW (example form 10-K)
  • cik/835403#id DIAGEO PLC
  • cik/1230276#id Pandora Media, Inc.
  • cik/1014931#id VULCAN VENTURES INC
  • cik/904057#id ALLEN PAUL G
  • cik/5272#id AMERICAN INTERNATIONAL GROUP INC
  • cik/831001#id CITIGROUP INC (example form 4)
  • ticker/GOOG#id GOOGLE INC
  • sic/ Standard Industry Code
  • cik/ Selected agent (organisations, persons) instances including links to filings.

Related: SEC data at rdfabout.com.
